### buffett-operating
The operational compendium of Berkshire's ~30+ major subsidiaries — BNSF Railway, Berkshire Hathaway Energy, See's Candy, Dairy Queen, Pampered Chef, Shaw, MiTek, Lubrizol, ISCAR, Precision Castparts, Nebraska Furniture Mart, Borsheims, Clayton Homes, NetJets, GEICO, Reinsurance. ### buffett-meetings
Full lifecycle of the Berkshire Hathaway annual meeting — origins, "Woodstock of Capitalism" (coined by Carol J. Loomis), preparation, Q&A format (Becky Quick), subsidiary shopping weekend, tone, notable exchanges, post-Munger era (2024).

### buffett-philanthropy
Most detailed treatment of Buffett's giving architecture — Gates Foundation relationship (2006), The Giving Pledge (2010, 250+ signatories), Susan Thompson Buffett Foundation, the children's foundations, personal giving philosophy.

### buffett-capitalism-politics
Buffett's political economy: the Buffett Paradox (2011: $39.8M income / $6.9M tax / 17.4% rate), the Buffett Rule, "Stop Coddling the Rich" (2012), estate tax advocacy, democracy/money critique.
