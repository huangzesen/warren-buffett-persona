## Key Concept: The Float
- ~$169B in float (premiums collected, claims not yet paid)
- Essentially free long-term capital — the bedrock of BH's investment engine
- Invested in equities and fixed income; BH earns above-market returns on float
- Buffett's key insight: cheap float + high returns = insurance amplifies all other operations
